8. Others

(1) Technical and Organizational Measures to Safeguard Personal Information

We have implemented the following technical and organizational measures to prevent personal information from being lost, stolen, leaked, altered, or damaged in the course of processing users' personal information.

-Internal Management Plan
. We have established and implemented an internal management plan for ensuring the secure processing of personal information.
. We have set up an division dedicated to privacy and data protection, which checks whether the concerned personnel comply with the safeguards for personal information and promptly take corrective measures upon detecting a problem.

- Access Control
. We control unauthorized access through an intrusion prevention system and seek to equip all technical devices in order to ensure security in the system.

- Prevention against Access Record Forgery and Alteration
. We keep and manage the access records of the personal information processing system and use the security features in order to prevent the access records from being forged or altered

- Encryption
. Your personal information is protected by passwords, stored, and managed after encrypting the files and transmitted data or locking the files. Important data is protected using additional security features.

- Prevention against Cyber Attacks
. We use antivirus software to prevent any cyber threat. Our antivirus program is regularly updated. In case of a new threat, we provide the antivirus solution the moment it is available in order to prevent personal data breaches.
. We adopt Secure Socket Layer (SSL), which ensures the secure transmission of personal information via the network by using an encryption algorithm.
. We utilize intrusion prevention systems and vulnerability assessment systems on each server in order to prevent cyberattacks.
. We do not keep personal information and general data together but store them separately using individual servers.

- Minimization and Education of Staff Who Handle User Information
. We grant access to users' personal information only to those who are involved in marketing activities dealing with customers directly, those who manage personal information, such as the Chief Privacy Officer and other relevant personnel, and those who inevitably handle personal information for business purposes.
. We provide regular education programs for employees who handle personal information both within the company and through outside professionals about new security technology and personal information protection obligations.
. We prevent human-caused data breaches by making all employees sign a confidentiality pledge before joining the company and putting in place internal procedures to audit compliance with the personal information protection measures by employees.
. The transfer of duties to successors from those who handle personal information is thoroughly conducted under high security, and we hold employees for any data incident responsible after joining or leaving the company.
. We designate computer rooms and data archives as restricted areas and control the access to them.

(2) Direct Marketing Communications
We do not send marketing messages for commercial purposes without your prior consent.
